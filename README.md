**A modern, feature-rich replacement for WoW's default Friends frame.**

BetterFriendlist completely overhauls your social experience in World of Warcraft. It combines powerful organization tools, advanced filtering, and modern customization options into a clean, native-feeling interface that fits perfectly with the modern WoW UI.

---

## ‚ú® Key Features

### üé® Modern Friend Management
*   **Custom Groups**: Create unlimited groups, rename them, and color-code them.
*   **Drag & Drop**: Easily move friends between groups or reorder groups.
*   **Favorites & "In-Game"**: Dedicated Favorites group and dynamic "In-Game" group for friends currently playing.
*   **Nicknames**: Assign custom nicknames to friends (separate from notes).
*   **Global Sync**: Automatically sync your friend groups across all your characters (Retail & Classic).

### üîç Advanced Sorting & Filtering
*   **Dual Sorting**: Sort by Status, Name, Level, Zone, Activity, Game, Faction, Guild, Class, or Realm.
*   **Quick Filters**: One-click filters for Online, Offline, Retail Only, Battle.net Only, and more.
*   **Smart Search**: Real-time search across names, characters, notes, and nicknames.

### üõ†Ô∏è Customization & Integration
*   **ElvUI Support**: Native skinning support for ElvUI users.
*   **Edit Mode**: Fully integrated with WoW's Edit Mode - move and resize the frame (50% - 200% scale).
*   **Name Formatting**: Customize how names appear using tokens like `%nickname%`, `%name%`, `%battletag%`.
*   **Data Broker**: Built-in LDB support for TitanPanel, Bazooka, and ChocolateBar.
*   **Raider.IO**: Seamless integration showing scores in tooltips.

### ‚öîÔ∏è Raid & Group Management
*   **Raid Frame**: Full 40-man raid management with drag & drop support.
*   **Role Management**: Assign Main Tank and Main Assist directly from the frame.
*   **Quick Join**: Enhanced interface for browsing and joining friends' groups.

### üìä Social Tools
*   **Who Frame**: Modernized "Who" list with advanced filtering and instant updates.
*   **Recent Allies**: Tracks players you've recently grouped with.
*   **Activity Tracking**: See when you last interacted with a friend (whisper, group, trade).
*   **Statistics**: View detailed stats about your friend list (Class distribution, Realms, etc.).
